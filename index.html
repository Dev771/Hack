Hack-BVICAM is a student run hackathon organized by BVICAM.
< MOTIVE > It devises to bring budding minds from across the country
         to compete and build hacks for problems they meet within day-to-day lives. < /MOTIVE >
>_ Teams of developers, designers, and innovators will come together to build prototypes of fully functional applications that tackle issues in our enlisted themes.
>_ Use this platform for the application of software and hardware technologies you have learned, get along with industry professionals and gain the experience they share!
< BVICAM > Hack-hackrcdu creates an environment that brings developers together irrespective of their gender, sexual orientation,              race/ethnicity, and socioeconomic status. < /BVICAM >
